Site Purpose

The site purpose is to promote a new way to enjoy the best produce from USA. The local hight quality produce is a healthy way to deal with the challenges of the fast pace and super active world living style. We provide a cool way to make orders through our site to enjoy the fresh produce and fruits to get your own designed drinks, Fruit bags, Vegetables, and more. We ship domestically and worldwide. We have the best industry standards to delivered controlled temperature and our promise of "high quality produce"

Target Audience and Scenarios

Scenarios

An interested visitor will come to our site to check with curiosity the freshness of our produce to buy a product from between a drink or order some for shipping.

An curious visitor will come from our social media to check the information shown in that. It is important to have strong relation between what we post and what we offer in our site. The main product we are going to post are a few drinks that shows the total values of carbohydrates, proteins, fat, calories ans sugar. Some of them are from our recipes, but visitors will have the option to prepare their drinks and check the total nutrition values before order them
