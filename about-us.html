Who Are We?

Bountiful foods is a 300-member company based in southern coastal area in California. It is owned by International Farming Corporation in North Carolina. Bountiful foods roots are in three former regional cooperatives: Trout, Inc., Blue Chelan, Inc., and Magi, Inc.

Saji Fruit and HRC orchards market the 4 to 5 million boxes produced annually at Bountiful foods' three plants.

Currently Bountiful foods receives and produces bins of conventional and organic grapes, apples, pears, cherries, and veggetables from 7,500 acres located in the souther coastal area in California. We employ 400 employees, peaking at 700 employees during grapes and cherry season.
